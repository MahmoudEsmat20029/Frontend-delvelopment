Instructions

  • Stand about 2-3 meters away from the screen.
  • Make sure the room is well-lit and avoid any reflections on the screen.
  • Wear your glasses if you need them.
  • Focus on the circle and try not to move your head while it appears.
  • Press the arrow corresponding to the direction of the gap in the circle: ↑ for up, ↓ for down, ← for left, → for right.
  • Each time you press an arrow, the next circle will appear slightly smaller.
  • Try to answer quickly and accurately, but do not rush before clearly seeing the circle.
  • After finishing the test, your final score will be displayed on the screen.
